Campus Features


Library

Our Library has a widespread variety of books. We have books that are nonfiction, fiction, informative, novels, and short stories. Our collections satisfy the interest of people in all ages in our school.



Multi-Purpose Room

The Multi-Purpose Room is a room that can be used for many things, as described in the name. On rainy days, P.E. can be held in it. Musical and vocal concerts and be stationed there too. Some events are hosted there as well. The Vocals also do there practice in the room too.



Event Center

The Event Center is a room that stands up for a lot more than its name. Sometimes, P.E. takes place there. Winds do their practice inside the Event Center. Certain events are hosted there. Class parties can be stationed there.
